📘 Project Documentation: AI-Driven Revenue Intelligence Engine (v6.3)

This document serves as the Implementation Guide and Reporting Framework for the Unified Intelligence Engine. It is designed for both the technical administrator and the business stakeholders to understand the system's architecture, value proposition, and operational requirements.1. Executive SummaryThe Unified Intelligence Engine (UIE) is a middleware solution built on Google Apps Script that transforms raw CRM data into actionable sales intelligence. By integrating HubSpot, MailUp, and Google Gemini 1.5 Flash, the system identifies "Hidden Revenue Signals"—high-intent leads that are incorrectly classified in the CRM—and automatically segments them for targeted marketing activation.2. System ArchitectureThe system operates as a 7-Phase Linear Pipeline, ensuring data integrity and cost-efficiency at every step.Phase 1 & 2 (Ingestion): Multi-threaded fetching of Contacts and Communications from HubSpot.Phase 3 (Aggregation): Lead-based deduplication to reduce AI processing volume.Phase 4 (Inference): Parallel AI analysis using Gemini to determine buying intent (1-100 score).Phase 5 & 6 (Intelligence): Dynamic segmentation and CRM "Gap Detection" (identifying high-intent "Cold" leads).Phase 7 (Activation): Pushing segmented leads to specific MailUp lists for personalized drip campaigns.3. Key Business Value MetricsFeatureBusiness ImpactGap DetectionIdentifies "Hidden Gems" (High AI Score + Cold CRM Status) for immediate sales follow-up.AI SegmentationAutomatically groups leads into categories (ERP, CRM, AI) for 1:1 personalization.Cost ControlAggregation logic reduces Gemini API usage by ~70% compared to signal-by-signal analysis.Fail-Safe ReliabilityImplements a Dead Letter Queue (DLQ) to ensure malformed data never breaks the pipeline.4.
